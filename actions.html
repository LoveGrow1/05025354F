Action recommendations

Involve your partner in decisions:

Engage 5025354M in discussions and decisions that affect you both. Encourage him to express his viewpoints openly and value his contribution. By doing this, he will feel more secure about the mutual benefit of his decisions.

Develop a 'no-judgement' communication zone:

Set aside regular times for open and honest conversation where both of you can express your needs and concerns without fear of a negative response. This will help both of you to communicate more freely.

Make emotional support more overt:

Given the demands of parenting and a relationship of 15 years, it's important to show appreciation for each other's support, especially during difficult times. Explicitly acknowledge his efforts and ask how he would prefer to be supported when he's upset. Demonstrating this in front of your kids can also be a wonderful example of emotional intelligence.
